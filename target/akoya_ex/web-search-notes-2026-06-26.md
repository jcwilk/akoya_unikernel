## Note on 2 GB maximum RAM

No additional Akoya EX–named page was found stating **2048 MB** maximum. The inventory uses the **Intel 855GM** GMCH limit (2 GB with high-density SO-DIMMs) chained to the review’s confirmation that the Akoya EX uses 855GM.
